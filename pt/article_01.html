Update report 01.05

01.05.2021


During this update; A lot of work has been done to modernize the interfaces, speed up the server and fix various bugs!


Alongside the fully updated Main Menu interface, we are introducing a change of the management interaction system!

Added a new user-friendly tablet interface for businesses. You can now track detailed statistics for each day and general statistics for 15 days. In addition, you can see the infographic revenues for each day for 15 days. The infographic update takes place at 00:00 Istanbul time.

Bug fix and increase in server performance:

  • Large-scale studies were carried out for mod optimization.
  • Fixed a bug that allowed you to exclude the family leader when offline.
  • Fixed a bug that did not update the amount of gasoline in the speedometer. It was necessary to get out of the car and get back in the car to see the amount of gas remaining.
  • The loading of the character has been accelerated. Now, when you log into the server, a data download takes place, which takes about 10 seconds.
  • Fixed a bug that prevented it from hiding the roulette bonus scroll UI in the settings.
  • Fixed a bug that did not correctly specify chat and hood time.
  • Fixed a bug that prevented the organization leader from being appointed.
  • Fixed an incorrect display of EXP count and character level.
  • Adjusted the tuning settings of some cars.
  • Fixed bugs in Checkpoints of some jobs. (Bus driver, package delivery, etc.)
  • Fixed an error when setting a new target on the map.
  • Fixed route error to deliver jobs on map.
  • Fixed trailer coupling error in product delivery job.
  • Fixed the bug of throwing from the car when interacting with the phone.
  • Fixed a bug that partially or completely corrupted the information on the tablet.
  • Fixed a bug that broke animations on the farm.
  • Fixed the issue where the required material would suddenly disappear while dealing with various tasks.
  • Fixed the bug that prevented the player from spinning the roulette.
  • Fixed phone balance loading error.
  • Fixed a bug where players could receive unlimited rewards during hospitalization.
